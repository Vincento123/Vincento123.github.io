Professional Profile

I am a Finance Analyst holding a Bachelor of Business Administration with IT (Accounting Option) from Maseno University, combining technical accounting expertise with technology fluency to deliver measurable value in fast-paced environments.

My career spans corporate finance, public-sector accounting, SME financial management, and community leadership — giving me a well-rounded, practical understanding of how organisations manage money at different scales and in different contexts.

At Digital Qatalyst, I manage end-to-end accounts payable, govern SaaS subscriptions, run full payroll cycles, and produce weekly and monthly management reports — all within Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and in strict compliance with IFRS, GAAP, and APQC frameworks.

My goal is to grow into a senior financial analyst or finance manager role, applying both my accounting foundations and technology capabilities to drive outstanding organisational outcomes.
